3000003. The Conquest of Tullaytillah and Córdoba

Granada — Not random

Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1819)

Description

In 1085 the city of Toledo was captured by a Christian army, and in 1236 Córdoba suffered the same fate.
