381000. Poland owns Prussia

Livonian Order — Not random

Conditions

  • Poland owns Prussia
  • None of the following must occur:
    • Teutonic Order exists
    • Prussia exists

Will happen within 60 days of January 2, 1419
Checked again every 60 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1660)

Description

The Teutonic Order was a always the main enemy of Poland. In 1410 at Tannenberg Wladyslaw Jagiello won a tremendous victory over the knights but failed to drive them out of Prussia. However, his successors have managed to accomplish that and Prussia is now in polish hands. What shall we do?

Actions

A. We must regain these lands!

  • -200 relations with Poland
  • +100 relations with Brandenburg
  • +100 relations with Austria
  • Memel will be considered a national province
  • Prussia will be considered a national province
  • Gain a temporary casus belli against Poland for 60 months
  • +5000 infantry in the capital province
  • +5000 cavalry in the capital province
  • +200

B. We have no interest in it

  • +300 relations with Poland
  • -50 relations with Brandenburg
  • -50 relations with Austria
  • Gain a temporary casus belli against Poland for 60 months
  • +100
